About Moscafe

Moscafe is a community-centered digital hub providing affordable internet access, business services, regulatory compliance assistance, and digital literacy support. Our mission is to create opportunities through technology.

High-speed internet access, printing, scanning and document services.

Digital Skills Support

Basic computer training and CV/application support for job seekers.
